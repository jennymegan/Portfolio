ABOUT ME

I’m a full-stack software developer with an interest in back-end development and machine learning. I graduated from Mayden Academy in December 2020.

Prior to my formal training I have almost a decade’s experience in project management and product design in a superyacht interior design company. My strengths are in my problem-solving and communication skills, both written and verbal.
